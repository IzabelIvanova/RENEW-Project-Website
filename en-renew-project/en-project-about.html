Project Goals

The main goal of RENEW is to create an intelligent electric vehicle charging system that utilizes solar energy and battery storage while ensuring flexible energy flow management.

Specific Objectives:

  • Integration of charging stations with solar panels and batteries
  • Development of an IoT-based platform for monitoring and management
  • Development of algorithms for energy forecasting and distribution
  • Optimization of the charging process based on grid load

Results

The expected results of the project include:

  • Creation of a smart energy system for electric vehicle charging
  • Development of an algorithm for optimizing power distribution
  • Software for managing and remotely controlling charging stations
  • Reduction of carbon emissions through increased renewable energy efficiency

Tasks

To achieve these results, the project includes the following key tasks:

  • Analysis of available technologies and best practices in smart grids
  • Development of hardware and software solutions for charging stations
  • Integration of IoT technologies for energy distribution management
  • Optimization of power distribution based on predictive algorithms
  • Testing and validation of developed solutions in real-world conditions
